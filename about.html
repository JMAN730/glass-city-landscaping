Our Story

Glass City Landscaping & Design was born in 2011 out of a passion for the Northwest Ohio landscape. Founded by the Miller family, we started with a single mower and a vision to provide the highest quality service in the Glass City.

Over the past 15 years, we've grown into a full-service design and maintenance company, but our core values haven't changed. We still treat every lawn as if it were our own and every client like a neighbor.

From the historic homes in Ottawa Hills to the modern developments in Perrysburg, we take pride in enhancing the beauty of our local communities.
